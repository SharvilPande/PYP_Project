About - HSS (Homeless Saviour Sqaud)



The HSS is a group of young kids (i.e. Sharvil, Conrad and Oliver) who want to support homeless people who are being majorly affected in the dangerous situation of COVID-19, so we've decided to set up a day for them where they can relax from their problems and ensure that they have a great time where they get to talk with other people and enjoy tasty food that we're keeping just for them in a buffet format. This will aim to make all those people feel less lonely and also support the cause of RUOK Day, where we'll be asking them Are You Ok and help brighten their life, where everything will be free. We will be passing the message via posters and contact homeless people by sponsoring with other homeless shelters.
